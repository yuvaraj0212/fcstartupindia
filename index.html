Atmanirbhar Bharat Abhiyaan(self-reliant)

The Self-Reliant India campaign is the vision of a new India envisioned by Shri Narendra Modi. During his address on 12 May 2020, our Prime Minister announced a special economic package worth Rs. 20 lakh crores - equivalent to 10% of Indian GDP - to fight the COVID-19 pandemic in India, and gave a kickstart to the Atmanirbhar Bharat Abhiyaan (Self-reliant India campaign).
He also outlined five pillars of Aatma Nirbhar Bharat Economy, Infrastructure, System, Vibrant Demography and Demand as the goal of achieving complete independence and self-reliance. Under the Aatmanirbhar Bharat Abhiyaan, the Finance Minister also announced Government Reforms and Enablers across seven sectors.
